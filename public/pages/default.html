Why

As a freelance I dont' know how my customers will behave with me, if it's going to be a good collaboration or if he is a very bad client.

From this simple necessity I've decided to build this web app where you can rate and review your customer, providing a link to the job you've done, a comment and a rating.

How

I've decided to provide to you a very simple sistem to rate and review your current, past and future customers. It's very easy: you log in with facebook or with you email and password and then you can review those customers.

Since your name would appear you have the responsability on what you'll write.

Disclaimer

The access to the site is free. I'm not responsable of the companies reviewed nor the reviews that are written.

In order to review a company you have to provide a project that you, as a freelance, have worked on. Without one you can't review anyone. This whole system leans on your honesty.

If someone reports your review I'll have to check if the review is real. If your review is proven fake, you'll have a warning: at the third youll'be banned and, of course, you'll not be able to review anybody.
